Dynasty Rankings Preseason 2022

Why does your team suck?

1. Evan's Pick Bank

After selling it all, its almost time to buy. But now is not the time, you need to buy when the market is low, and just before the season is not the time. Everyone thinks their team is a contender. I like the young WR talent, and once RB’s join this roster there will be chance. Let’s see what happens with all the picks.

2. I’m Hard Man

Has shown a lot of potential over the years but is always devasted by injury or trades. Acquiring the King may be what finally turns the L into W’s. But there is a lot of old and risky players with a lot of injury.

3. T-marsh

Another team following suit with the injury bug. As Henry was traded last year to I’m Hard Man in order to make a playoff push but then James Robinson got hurt as well. OBJ is still recovering, Zeke was injured all last year, Adams to the Raiders, there is a lot of questions around key players that could propel this team back to the top or tank and burn. Three players on this roster are either retired or do not have jobs, not a good look for a previous champion.

4. Stop giving A$$ Trades

This feels too low of a ranking for a team with Cooper Kupp, but last year was record breaking. It will not happen again. The wide receivers are all overall great but took hits all around. Waddle added Tyreek, Moore added Wilson, Hopkins got suspended. The hope is for injuries in the DEN and SEA backfield to better his RBs.

5. Rely on the C’s

CMC is back, hopefully and that’s about it. If CMC comes back to full strength and workload he is worth 1.5 times Jonathan Taylors but it’s a hope. There is a lot of potential here with young talent that can quickly return value, but its unknown still and hard to rank as a playoff team yet.

6. Monaghan Manor

A few nice additions here, Chubb is one of the best pure runners in the league. Russ to Denver, Brady unretires, Julio with the Bucs, ARob to LA, and Mooney the lone guy in Chicago. Kamara suspension likely pushed back a year. I think a third top rb is needed to push Manor over the edge, hopefully before this team all decides to retire.

7. Token Cake Eater

I think selling Elijah Mitchell was good value, for next years draft, but that only hurts him now. Cam akers looked back in the playoffs, and may not get the full workload, Breece Hall is on the Jets, AJ Dillon will keep splitting with Aaron Jones and may lose valuable goal line and receiving work. But if I am wrong on those, all other talent is great to elite.

8. Chasin’ Lambs

I think the weakness here is in the RBs. But they are pretty underrated and may provide nice value. The issue is Montgomery is on the crappy bears, and Mitchell is in the San Fransico RB committee that changes so often. Lamb has his chance to be a WR 1, Hollywood can be a one while Hopkins is out. A playoff push can be done with 2 tier RB’s.

9. Treechi1d’s

RB’s and WRs are very good. Cook had a disappointing year last year with no TD’s, and Jones may say every packers target this year without Adams (see SF playoff game targets). The question is if Deshaun Watson will be suspended. It doesn’t sound good. I think Roger Goodell just said he wants a full season suspension. But even without Watson, a Derek Carr may be able to help this team compete.

10. Vacation Funds

Probably the lowest ranking I have finally been able to give him. Usually the high ranking is due to respect for winning the previous year, but It feels like there are a surprising amount of rising questions. How long will Patterson stay healthy? We all were in disbelief from last years performance. Will Dobbins be healthy? It feels like it. Who will be Russel Wilson’s WR 1, Jeudy or Sutton. Jeudy has had two years and hasn’t shown much. Was St. Browns miracle las 6 games of the season only due to Hockenson and Swift being out for a majority of that? What happens when they are all back. And finally, will Zeke be missed? He was injured all of last year and was still the RB 5. That’s a lot of production to take out of a roster.

11. Mahome Boy’s

The stack still lives, Mahomes to Moore, or maybe Juju or MVS. But besides that overall great team except at TE. Mixon, Fat Fournette, and Conner, all three were top 10 RB’s. JJ Hill were also top 10 at the position. The question is a lot of these players are not being ranked to repeat last years great performances.

12. All Pitts Stink

Hurts get AJ Brown, win. Pittman get Matty Ice. CEH loses Darrel Williams. Diontae Johnson signs 2 year deal. Saquon is healthy. Penny is healthy. Godwin is practicing. Really the only disappointment was Calvin Ridley’s suspension.
